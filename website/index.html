About Neckare

Neckare is your companion in your journey towards better posture and habits. We aim to help you build better habits by reminding you to do your stretches. These simple stretches take fewer than 3 minutes but can prevent a lifetime of bad posture and chronic back pain.

Video Demonstration

Here is how you do the exercise and earn Posture Points(PP):

  1. Sit relaxed in your neutral position
  2. Tuck your chin in and lean back from your shoulders and above
  3. Lean your head, neck and shoulders back as much as you can
  4. Feel the stretch in your back and the tension in your neck being relieved
  5. Repeat this action at your own time and pace 5 times
  6. Repeat these steps every 2 hours of computer desk use or when you feel tension in your neck
